HOW IT WORKS

We take you on a journey (oil journey), how oil from the rig translates to projects in your community. We give you the power
to know what the government is doing with your money. We give you the power to ask
questions and demand answers


  • Know how much money was made this year

    We inform you the amount of money the government made from oil every year.

  • Know what projects the money went into

    We inform you about the project that the govenment undertakes with this money.

  • Supervise and confirm projects

    You can confirm if this this project is taken place or not.

  • Review the work done with the money

    Check if the work done shows value for money.

  • Ask Question and Demand Answers

    Finally, we give you the opportunity to ask questions, thereby allowing you to get involved and make your voices count.
